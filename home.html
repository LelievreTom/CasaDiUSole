Welcome to the guest rooms of Casa di u Sole

Independent Room with a Clear View of the Maquis, Sea, and Mountains

Treat yourself to a peaceful getaway in an ideal villa for recharging, located in an idyllic, calm, and soothing setting, just 10 minutes from the center of Porto-Vecchio and 5 minutes from the most beautiful beaches in southern Corsica. This independent, spacious, and bright room comes with its own private bathroom to ensure your comfort and privacy. It also has parking, private access, air conditioning, and a terrace with a heated pool (loungers and towels provided).

Enjoy a private terrace where you can relax while taking in the breathtaking view of the surrounding maquis and the sea. The lush garden and pool will allow you to escape into a peaceful, green environment. An outdoor kitchen, fully equipped, is available for you to prepare your meals freely. Whether you are seeking tranquility or an authentic experience, this room in a private home is the perfect choice for a pleasant and relaxing stay.

À votre disposition, une cuisine extérieur équipée (Cafetière, bouilloir, grille pain, presse agrumes, frigo) vous permettra de préparer vos petits-déjeuners en toute liberté. Que vous soyez en quête de tranquillité ou d'une expérience authentique, cette chambre chez l'habitant est le choix parfait pour un séjour agréable et reposant.

We are deeply in love with the wild and preserved beauty of Corsica, an island where nature reveals itself in all its splendor. From the majestic mountains and secret coves to the beaches with crystal-clear waters, every corner of the island invites you to escape and explore. We highly recommend discovering these unique landscapes through beautiful hikes, which will take you through lush forests and along picturesque coastlines. Whether you are passionate about hiking or simply seeking a peaceful setting to recharge, Corsica offers a multitude of trails that perfectly combine sea and mountain. An unforgettable experience awaits at every turn!
